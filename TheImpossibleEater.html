Eggs and raisins?! Okay, I know it sounds gross, but bear with me. This twist on the classic “two-ingredient” banana-egg pancakes takes less than 5 minutes to whip together for an easy protein-packed meal! Ingredients: 1 banana 2 eggs dash (or two) cinnamon 1-2 TBS raisins *flax meal *peanut butter to top Directions: Mash banana in a bowl and beat in eggs. Add cinnamon, flax, and any other add ins (chia, protein powder, etc.). Scramble over low-medium heat and add in raisins as desired. Cook until just dry. Enjoy!
